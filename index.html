Why You Should Buy a Jeep Wrangler

 

  1. Pros of a Wrangler
    • Parts are relatively inexpensive and widely available
    • The drivetrains are very reliable once a few known issues are addressed

       

  2. Cons of a Wrangler
    • Rides noticably worse on the road than other cars
    • Does not get great fuel economy or have good handling

       

  3. Things a Wrangler Can Do That Other Vehicles Can't
    • Take the top off
    • Crawl over very uneven terrain
    • Survive a fender bender with minimal damage
    • Other Wrangler owners will wave at you
    • Ford very deep water
    • Has drains in the floorpan to wash out the interior
    • Has an infinite aftermarket support for modifications
